About us



MISSION

Find plasma donors for your or your loved ones' plasma therapy against COVID-19.

Our team created a website to connect current coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) patients with recovered ones who wish to donate their plasma for plasma therapy. We are committed to our goal of amassing a large public dataset of willing donors through publicly available data and our own donor-registration functionality. Donors can register by agreeing to donate their blood – we ask them for details not limited to name, contact information, date and proof of recovery, blood group, other medical conditions (comorbidities) etc. We extensively vet any donor registering through us to the best of our abilities.


What sets us apart from other plasma donation dashboards is our open source nature. The information about the donors is listed on our website. Critical patients and their loved ones don’t have to wait for match-making and can directly contact eligible patients on urgent basis. We ensure privacy of our kind donors by protecting all their non-contact-related information.
