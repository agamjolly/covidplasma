FAQ

Find answers to the questions most frequently asked from us.



Q1. How do you ensure the privacy of your donors?

To ensure the privacy of our donors, we do not share private information such as name, email or address publicly. While we do *however* recommend our donor’s to make their phone numbers public for the patients to be able to contact the donors. If you’re a donor and wish to have your phone concealed, you can choose so while signing up as a donor. If you’re a patient and want to contact a donor who doesn’t have their contact information public, please see question 3.


Q2. Is everything verified on your website?

Each donor’s information card states whether their information has been verified on our end or not. Even for verified donors, patients in need of plasma are required to perform their own verification.


Q3. How do I reach a donor who doesn’t have their contact information public?

You can write to us at ___(fill email)___ or ____(social media handles)____ and we will contact those donors ourself. We will keep you updated on the donor’s responses, someone from our team is involved in such communication 24 hours a day. We understand how urgent the sphere of plasma and blood donation is, therefore we will try our best to reach back to you in the earliest possible time.
